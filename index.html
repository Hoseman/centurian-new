About Centurian Automotive in Northamptonshire

Every aspect of our business is extraordinary and without compromise.

Our stunning showroom is nestled in 150 acres of beautiful Northamptonshire countryside, 1 mile from J12 of the A14, 10 mins from the A1 and 50 mins from Kings Cross Station by train.

We offer a free chauffeur collection service from Kettering station and Stansted Airport.

Centurian’s ethos and approach has evolved over 20 years of experience the standards we have learnt and adhere to have no compromise.

Each and every one of our beautiful examples are hand picked by our Founder Paul Harding (fortunately Paul has good taste) independently inspected and sold to our guaranteed retail standard.

We supply 12 months clean MOT, the remainder of manufacturers warranty or 6 months award winning warranty, which is extendable for up to 3 years. Tires will have no less that 3mm tread, brake discs and pads will be no more than 70% worn. We will check all outstanding recalls, look after cherished transfers, and finally we finish every car with a full Meguiars Clay Bar, professional polish and detail.

We have the ultimate product, the ultimate customer service levels, and our in-house Barista Paul makes fantastic coffee, Caramel Latte and Ethiopian yirgacheffe Espresso.

We also offer a delivery service anywhere in mainland UK.
